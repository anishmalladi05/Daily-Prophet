Breaking News! Muggles replicate Cauldrons, drive local shops out of business!

Witches and wizards across the nation are waking up to a startling new reality: their household items are multiplying without a single Geminio charm. The Department of Magical Accidents and Catastrophes has confirmed that a rogue batch of "3D-Printed" pewter cauldrons has been circulating in Diagon Alley. These vessels, crafted using a bizarre Muggle technique of "additive layer manufacturing" and "3D scanners" are seemingly capable of extruding perfect replicas of themselves when exposed to standard potion-brewing temperatures. "It's absolute chaos," reported one frantic shopkeeper at Potage's Cauldron Shop. "I left one on the shelf, and by morning there were fifty! They don't even need a wand! Just some sort of plastic filament and an "STL file"!" The Ministry is advising all citizens to check their cauldrons for "layer lines" and to report any unauthorized replication immediately.
